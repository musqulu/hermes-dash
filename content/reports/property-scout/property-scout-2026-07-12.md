- **Siemczyno:** roughly 73 PLN/m² land and 822 PLN/m² building area if the stated 669 m² total buildings is real. Ask for monument-conservator scope, roof photos, damp/structure state, septic/water/electricity details, access road status, pond ownership/rights, and operating limits for agro/stables.
- **Długie:** roughly 51 PLN/m² land. Best value-per-land among the accessible house leads, with utilities better than many rural listings. Main risk is shared/partial property: demand KW, cadastral map, division/use agreement, access to all buildings, and whether the second barn/remains create demolition liability.
- **Brody 415k:** about 98 PLN/m² land. MPZP MN is a meaningful positive if confirmed. Ask gmina about minimum plot size, road access for the proposed splits, utility capacity, and whether the 2027 bridge claim matters for actual resale.
- **Żydowo:** about 48 PLN/m² land on headline numbers, but co-ownership reduces control. Before viewing, ask what exactly is owned: separate KW share, exclusive-use agreement, plot map, mortgage/encumbrances, year-round access, and lake access rights.
- **Łobozew Dolny:** high land metric at about 268 PLN/m² and tiny building, so only interesting if Konrad wants a ready Bieszczady base; not a land-bank lead.

OLX and Otodom pages were reachable as HTML; the useful candidate facts above come mainly from Otodom regional result pages with price, location, house size and some land-size claims visible in snippets/cards.

Best buyer-side candidate today is **Bronikowo**: 1.03 ha plus a 100 m² house for 290k PLN gives the best land-to-price asymmetry in the surfaced set. It still needs the standard rural checks: KW ownership, road status, MPZP/WZ, media, septic/well legality, roof/damp/structure, and whether agricultural restrictions affect non-farmer purchase or future building.

For every lead, ask before viewing: exact plot number and KW, land classes, MPZP/WZ status, public-road access or easement, utilities on plot vs nearby, septic/well documents, recent roof/heating/electric work, flood/wetland risk, and whether any outbuilding is illegal or protected. Negotiation should lean on renovation unknowns, rural liquidity, and any vague land/access/media wording.

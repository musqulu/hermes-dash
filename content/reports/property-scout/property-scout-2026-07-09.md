## Short evidence and diligence notes

Best buyer-quality lead today is **Racław**: the land-to-price ratio, pond, and outbuilding mass give optionality for a personal retreat, workshop, animals or small agrotourism. The risk is that a cheap large homestead often hides roof/damp/septic/access issues, so do not view emotionally before documents.

For houses, ask for KW number, plot numbers, building condition disclosures, heating/septic/water/electric details, roof age, damp photos, and confirmation of legal access from a public road. For land, verify MPZP/WZ scope, soil/forest classes, flood/wetland/protected-area overlays, utility connection cost, and whether access is public road or easement.

Negotiation leverage is strongest on unfinished renovations, oversized outbuildings, unclear planning, and agency listings that have broad lifestyle language but thin hard facts. Racław, Dubiecko, Siemczyno and Osowa Sień should be approached document-first; Rokitki and Stare Bogaczowice should be checked at the gmina before any viewing.

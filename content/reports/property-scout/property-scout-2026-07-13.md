## Short evidence notes

Searches checked focused Polish rural queries and accessible Otodom/OLX result pages for Lubuskie, Zachodniopomorskie and Dolnośląskie. Otodom surfaced several attractive nature/water listings, but many were far above the ~600k PLN target and were rejected. OLX provided usable snippets for several sub-600k village properties. The strongest buyer-side fit today is not a polished “dream house” but practical land-plus-buildings where ugly condition, shared-title risk, or unclear documents may create negotiation leverage.

## Diligence priorities

Ask every seller for KW number, ownership structure, exact plot numbers, MPZP/WZ status, building legality, road access basis, utilities, septic/sewage, roof age, damp/structural problems and whether any outbuildings are legal or demolition liabilities. For half-house/co-owned properties, do not visit until the division, exclusive-use areas and land access are clear in documents.

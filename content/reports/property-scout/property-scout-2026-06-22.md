- **Verdict:** strong lead, but only if the “half of house / half of barn” ownership setup is clean.
- **Link:** https://adresowo.pl/o/dom-szprotawa-dlugie-95-m2-t8b0w7
- **Location / region:** Długie, near Szprotawa / Lubuskie.
- **Key numbers:** 314,000 PLN; 95 m² house; 6.1 ha land; roughly **51,500 PLN/ha** and **3,305 PLN/m²** of house. Listing says water, collective sewage, three-phase electricity, year-round access; gas near plot boundary.
- **Why it’s interesting:** The land-to-price ratio is the standout today. 6.1 ha with residential/building components below 320k PLN is rare enough to investigate, especially with utilities already mentioned. Outbuildings/stodola give workshop, storage, animal, or future retreat utility.
- **Red flags / unknowns:** “Half of house”, “half of barn” can be a legal mess: co-ownership, physical division, shared roof/walls, unclear access, and future conflict risk. Condition/roof/damp unknown. Land classes, MPZP/WZ, flood/wetland and whether all 6.1 ha is usable are unknown.
- **Questions to ask seller / municipality:** Is it a separate KW or udział/co-ownership? Is there a notarial/use division of house, yard, barn and driveway? Exact plot numbers and land classes? MPZP or WZ? Any leases/dzierżawa? Can all buildings be legally used/renovated? Any moisture, roof, septic/sewage documentation?
- **Next action:** Call owner first. Do not view emotionally before KW/plot numbers and ownership structure are clear.

## Short evidence notes

- **Moracz-Derkacz:** listing says capital renovation, new roof, water and electricity, 40 km to the sea, near S3, river boundary; ask for KW, exact flood map status, septic/sewage, structural damp, roof invoices, and year-round road status.
- **Gołaszyn:** two KW numbers are provided; seller is an institutional company, which may help document flow, but the building stock is large and specialised; ask for building condition, asbestos, environmental liability, utilities, and whether residential conversion is realistic.
- **Studzieniec / Stary Waliszów:** both are primarily agricultural land-bank plays; do not assume cabins or natural building rights before checking MPZP/Plan Ogólny/WZ and KOWR/agricultural purchase constraints.
- **Studzienno:** strongest planning clarity among the land leads because MPZP is stated; verify exact plan sheet, road status, utilities distance, slope/exposure, and whether tourism use permits cabins or only guest accommodation tied to a main building.
- **Zdroisko:** direct forest adjacency is attractive, but national-road adjacency can kill retreat value; inspect noise, access geometry, and whether R/Lzr land can actually be developed without costly exclusions.

Key diligence questions for any call: exact land area and plot numbers, KW ownership and encumbrances, MPZP/WZ and whether residential/recreational use is allowed, public road vs easement, year-round access, electricity/water/septic status, roof/damp/heating condition, flood/wetland/protected-area constraints, and nearest real services.

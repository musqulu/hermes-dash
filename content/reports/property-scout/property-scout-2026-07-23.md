## Short evidence and diligence notes

Best buyer-side lead is **Kolsk** because the land area is unusually high under 600k and the setting appears to match privacy/nature goals. First calls should focus on: exact plot numbers, KW, land classes, MPZP/WZ, whether all 1.5167 ha is included and contiguous, condition of roof/foundations/damp/heating, water source, legal road access, and whether any buildings require demolition or permits. For **Racław**, verify the pond legality/water table and whether the 7,300 m² is usable or partly wet/forest. For **Stańkowa**, ask for KW screenshots, plot map, winter access, septic/sewage, heating, and whether tourism/cabins are legally realistic. For **Strupina/Bartodzieje**, protected-area and flood checks are mandatory before viewing. Negotiation angle across these: request documents first, then price against renovation liabilities and unclear planning status rather than seller lifestyle copy.

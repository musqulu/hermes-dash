## Evidence and diligence notes

- Adresowo category pages were accessible; DuckDuckGo search returned an anomaly/challenge page, and Brave later rate-limited after initial usable results. I therefore used search-result links plus direct Adresowo listing extraction.
- **Stołuń:** ask seller for KW, exact plot IDs, forest/grassland classifications, whether any part can legally host siedlisko/buildings, road legal status, Natura/protected-area constraints, and forest management plan obligations. Negotiation: above-budget all-cash/conditional offer only after confirming building optionality; otherwise price it as regulated forest/meadow land, not lifestyle land.
- **Radęcin:** verify road status, plot IDs, forest restrictions, no hidden easement-only access, and whether utilities are realistically nearby. Negotiation: comparable to Stołuń but with less meadow/building upside, so do not chase if seller prices it as future development.
- **Guzów:** request roof photos, damp/structural inspection, heating/chimney status, septic documentation, water rights/connection confirmation, KW and any renovation permits. Negotiation: listing says cash buyers welcome and price negotiable; use renovation uncertainty and small land size.
- **Brody:** listing states LsV forest classification and plot ID; check whether any clearing/building is impossible, because forest land is often only recreation/ownership, not development.
- **Dobrocin:** ask for MPZP excerpt, media connection conditions, exact boundaries, slope/drainage, and whether “water/prąd in boundary” means signed connection terms or only nearby networks.

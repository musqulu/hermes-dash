## Short evidence notes

- Best land-value lead is Jarszewo: 49,200 m² at about 6.10 PLN/m², but it is agricultural, so the entire thesis depends on buildability and access documents.
- Best personal retreat lead is Skrzynka/Lipiany: sub-150k entry price, near lakes/forest, and some stated siedlisko potential; legal wording is messy and must be verified before emotional interest.
- Best mountain/nature house lead is Proszowa, but at 549k PLN it needs clean documents and usable land, not just pretty surroundings.
- Posada is the renovation gamble: cheap enough to inspect, but not safe to price without roof/structure/damp/utilities proof.
- Turzany was reviewed but not recommended above the others: 440,000 PLN for 4,712 m² with outbuildings/238 m² house gives rural character, but land is relatively small for the price and due-diligence burden remains high.

## Questions to ask first

- Send KW number, cadastral plot numbers, land classes, MPZP extract or WZ decision, and confirmation of legal road access.
- Confirm exact utilities: electricity connection status, water source, sewage/septic, and whether utilities are on the plot or only nearby.
- For houses: ask for roof age/material, damp, foundations, heating, windows, insulation, structural cracks, and whether any asbestos/eternit exists.
- For land-only/siedlisko claims: ask what document proves buildability, not just the seller’s statement.

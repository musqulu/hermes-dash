## Seller/gmina questions before viewing

Ask for KW number, cadastral plot IDs, MPZP extract or WZ decision, land classes including Ls/Ł/Ps/R, exact road access status, utilities map, flood/wetland/protected-area overlays, and whether any lease, ARiMR obligations, easements, pre-emption rights or neighbour disputes exist.

# One property worth opening today

[Rusinowo, gmina Świdwin, Zachodniopomorskie — 230,000 PLN for a 1930 detached house of 160 m² on 5,048 m² land](https://adresowo.pl/o/dom-swidwin-rusinowo-rusinowo-t2a7v2) looks like the cleanest buyer-side lead today because it is direct-from-owner, newly added, negotiable, and priced around 45.56 PLN/m² of land / 1,438 PLN/m² of building.

- Interesting because the land is meaningful for a low-budget rural base: 0.5 ha is not a true large siedlisko, but enough for privacy, garden/orchard/workshop use if zoning and neighbours cooperate.
- The listing states building condition is **do remontu**; that is leverage, but also the main cost risk — assume roof, damp, structure, heating, electrics and sewage all need verification before emotional commitment.
- Known facts from the open listing: plot no. 54/1, obręb 0027, area 5,048 m², house built in 1930, price negotiable, direct owner; access/utilities/MPZP/KW/flood risk remain unknown from the source.

Caveat: I checked Adresowo regional pages plus search queries for Lubuskie, Zachodniopomorskie, Dolnośląskie and Bieszczady-style siedliska, but no `web_search` tool is exposed in this cron environment, so the research used direct HTTP/search-page retrieval through terminal rather than a native web_search call.

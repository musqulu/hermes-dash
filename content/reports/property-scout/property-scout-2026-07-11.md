## Short evidence and buyer notes

- Main source used today was OLX listing state from regional siedlisko searches under 600k PLN; Siedliska Online was checked but target-region results were thin or outside the buy box.
- The two most asymmetric land-price leads are **Dubiecko** and **Kościelniki Górne**; both require careful MPZP/WZ, land-class, forest/protected-area and access verification before emotional interest.
- For every serious call, ask for: KW number, parcel IDs, MPZP/WZ extract, exact land area split, media and access-road status, septic/water legality, roof/structure photos, damp history, and whether any buildings are under conservation/protection.
- Negotiation leverage is strongest where listings show renovation burden, inconsistent land data, conservator restrictions, old heating/septic, or large buildings that create liability rather than usable value.

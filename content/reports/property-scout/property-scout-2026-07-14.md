## Questions to ask before viewing

- Send KW number, plot numbers, cadastral map and proof of ownership.
- Is there MPZP; if yes, exact designation, or if no, any WZ history?
- Confirm legal access from public road, not only customary use.
- Confirm utilities with documents: electricity capacity, water source, sewage/septic permit, heating type.
- For old houses: roof age, damp in walls/foundations, structural cracks, chimney condition, asbestos, insulation, windows.
- For water/staw/strumyk listings: flood maps, drainage, wetlands/protected-area restrictions.
- For agency listings: total buyer commission and whether exact address/docs are available before signing anything.
